## 🧠 THE PHILOSOPHY
DisciplineOS isn't a "to-do list." It is a behavioral modification system. It treats your life like a high-stakes RPG where every win earns XP and every slip-up has a cost. Built for those who want to transition from **Beginner** to **Monk** mode.

---

## 🔥 CORE ENGINE FEATURES

### ⚡ Smart Task System
* **Daily Missions:** Hand-picked tasks for growth (Deep Work, Reading, Detox).
* **XP Leveling:** Earn +50 XP for every task completed. Level up your rank from *Starter* to *Beast*.
* **Streak Protection:** Visual fire icons to track your consistency. Break the streak, lose the momentum.

### ⚔️ Urge Battle Mode (Emergency Protocol)
* **Tactical Response:** A dedicated "Urge" button for when cravings hit.
* **Actionable Alternatives:** Instant suggestions like "10 Pushups" or "Cold Water Face Wash" to break the dopamine loop.

### 📊 "No-Delusion" Analytics
* **Performance Graph:** A visual timeline of your growth. If the line goes down, you're falling behind.
* **Movement Log:** Simple toggle for *Light* or *Full* workouts. No zero days.
* **Weekly Grid:** A Mon-Sun view of your discipline.
